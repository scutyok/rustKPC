These nuggets of crystallized fury will assemble into a mighty Rage Orb once a full forty have been found. The diamonds can be activated before turning into a Rage Orb, but will yield less impressive results if spent prematurely. They often lie scattered in hidden nooks and crannies so be on the lookout.
